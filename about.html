About Us

INDTEK EQUIPMENTS AND CALIBRATION SERVICES LLP (IEC)

Established in 2013, serving with precision and trust.

INDTEK EQUIPMENTS AND CALIBRATION SERVICES LLP (IEC) was established in the year 2013, initially under the name Indian Technologies. With a vision to expand and streamline its operations in the field of testing equipment and calibration services, the company was later restructured and renamed as INDTEK EQUIPMENTS AND CALIBRATION SERVICES LLP. Over the years, IEC has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, accurate, and professional services in the domain of material testing and sand testing equipment.

From its inception, IEC has focused on combining technical expertise with customer-centric solutions. The company began with four partners, laying the foundation for its operations and growth. After two years, two partners retired from the company. Despite this transition, the remaining two partners demonstrated commitment and resilience by continuing the business as both managing and working partners. Their determination and consistent efforts ensured that the company not only sustained but also gradually strengthened its market presence.

In 2025, the partnership structure was further amended to include three partners, marking another important milestone in the company‚Äôs journey. With this revised structure, IEC continues to operate successfully in the field of sales, service, and on-site calibration of testing equipment. The company has steadily expanded its client base, ensuring long-term relationships built on trust, professionalism, and technical excellence.

To ensure the highest standards of calibration, IEC operates a Site Calibration Laboratory (SCL), which functions as a dedicated department under its structure. The SCL is managed under the direct supervision of the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), who is responsible for overseeing all laboratory functions, ensuring compliance with technical standards, and maintaining quality assurance. The lab is staffed with highly trained professional engineers who bring expertise and precision to the calibration process.

Beyond calibration, IEC also provides comprehensive services for a wide variety of testing machines. This includes preventive maintenance, breakdown services, and technical consultation, thereby offering end-to-end support to clients. By integrating sales, service, and calibration, IEC positions itself as a complete solutions provider in the testing equipment industry.

With over a decade of experience, a strong team of experts, and a commitment to innovation, IEC continues to grow as a trusted partner in the industry. Its dedication to quality, accuracy, and customer satisfaction makes it a preferred choice for organizations seeking reliable equipment solutions and calibration services.

Quality Policy

INDTEK EQUIPMENTS AND CALIBRATION SERVICES LLP (IEC) is fully committed to delivering high-quality services in the areas of testing equipment, calibration, and customer support. We constantly strive to enhance our capabilities by adopting the latest technologies, techniques, and best practices to meet and exceed customer requirements and expectations.

IEC complies with ISO/IEC 17025 and other relevant quality management systems by upholding good professional practices and maintaining integrity, impartiality, transparency, and credibility in all our services. We are committed to sustaining competence and ensuring consistent operation of all laboratory activities.
