About INDTEK

Indtek is a trusted name in engineering equipment, known for delivering high-precision material and sand testing solutions across Southern India. Our Professional Services

Indtek specializes in the sales, installation, calibration, and maintenance of advanced material and sand testing equipment. Our product portfolio includes universal testing machines, tensile testing instruments, Rockwell, Brinell, and Vickers hardness testers, impact testers compliant with ISO/ASTM, and portable hardness testers with NIST traceability.
