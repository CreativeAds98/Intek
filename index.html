Our Professional Services

Indtek specializes in the sales, installation, calibration, and maintenance of advanced material and sand testing equipment.

Our range covers universal and tensile testing machines, hardness testers (Rockwell, Brinell, Vickers), impact testers compliant with ISO/ASTM standards, and portable hardness testers with NIST traceability.

Backed by a service team trained to manufacturer standards and equipped with extensive field experience, we ensure precise calibration and dependable after-sales support‚Äîkeeping your equipment performing at its best.

Sand Testing Equipment

Sand testing involves analyzing the physical, chemical, and mechanical properties of sand to determine its suitability for various industrial applications, particularly within foundries and casting operations.

Material Testing

Material testing evaluates the physical, chemical, mechanical, and thermal properties of materials like metals, plastics, concrete, and ceramics to assess their reliability, performance, and suitability for specific applications.

NABL Accreditation

The National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) ensures that testing and calibration labs comply with global quality standards like ISO/IEC 17025. NABL accreditation promotes accuracy, reliability, and international acceptance of laboratory results.
