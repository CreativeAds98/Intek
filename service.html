Material Testing Machines

  • Universal Testing Machine: Versatile equipment testing tensile, compression, and flexural properties of various materials.
  • Tensile Testing Machine: Measures the force required to pull materials to their breaking point.
  • Rockwell Hardness Testing Machine: Rapidly determines surface hardness using standardized scale methods.
  • Brinell Hardness Testing Machine: Provides hardness readings through precise indentation, suitable for metals and alloys.
  • Vickers Hardness Testing Machine: Tests micro and macro hardness with high accuracy, ideal for thin samples and coatings.
  • Impact Testing Machine - ISO / ASTM: Assesses material toughness and energy absorption under impact to global standards.
  • Portable Hardness with NIST Traceability: Mobile device for on-site hardness checks, results traceable to NIST certification.

Sand Testing Equipment

  • Universal Strength Machine: Tests compressive and transverse strength of foundry sand mixtures.
  • Core Hardness Tester: Measures sand core integrity for high-quality casting results.
  • Mould Hardness Tester: Evaluates surface firmness of molded sand to ensure proper casting surfaces.
  • Permeability Meter: Analyzes air and gas permeability of sand samples for optimal metal flow control.
  • Sand Rammer: Standardizes sand compaction for consistent sample preparation.
  • Wet Tensile Tester: Determines tensile strength of green sands when wet, critical for process control.
  • Auto Clay Washer: Automates the washing of sand for accurate clay content measurement.
  • Core Gas Determinator: Quantifies gas evolution from sand cores to minimize casting defects.
  • Hot Tensile Tester: Evaluates sand strength at elevated temperatures for quality assurance.
  • VCAT - Online Testing for Green Sand: Real-time sand property analysis for optimized foundry operations.
